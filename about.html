R35 GTR Skyline Overview

The R35 GTR Skyline is a true icon in the world of high-performance sports cars. Known for its cutting-edge technology, powerful engine, and distinctive design, the R35 GTR continues the legacy of the Skyline series. With features such as the advanced ATTESA E-TS all-wheel-drive system and the potent VR38DETT engine, this car delivers a thrilling driving experience.
